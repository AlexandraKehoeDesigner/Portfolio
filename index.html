About Me!

I am from a diverse background, one with roots in marketing, communication, sales and managing. This gave me perspective with collaborating, branding communications, observing and researching human interactions, managing and organizing marketing research, and more. The combination of my years spent on all this lead to my passion for UX/ UI Designing.

My approach in creating a design strategy is by finding a balance between the user, looks, and desired expectations. I understand no two designs are the same and each design takes creative problem solving as well as inspiration to create. I derive my inspiration from the people around me. Whether I'm surrounded by friends or in a coffee shop surrounded by strangers I'm always observing and learning. This is what motivates me to design digital products that are user friendly and influences interactions.
